About Us

The history of Hoof 'n' Horn is an intriguing, electrifying, and rousing affair that stretches back many decades to the first production in 1936 of a student-written show called The Devil Grins.

The organization currently puts on three mainstage and one or two smaller productions every year.We are currently in the midst of a project to consolidate our history into a single volume so that everyone can know, among other things, why our rehearsal space is now called "Phred." There are many gaps in our knowledge. For instance, most of our current members have no idea that the Celestial Omnibus was once a part of HnH.

Traditions like Quarters and The Game are always introduced to new members, but the history behind them is often confusing or innaccurate. Once you know the real story behind something in HnH, however, you have become part of our inner circle. We want to include more people in this circle, so we will continue our compilation of knowledge and the results of our efforts will be available here.
