The Drowsy Chaperone

Show Info

Have you got that 20s bug? Do you wish you could be transported back to a time of extravagant parties and bobbed hair? Then please come join us as the Man in Chair leads us through the record of his favorite musical, The Drowsy Chaperone, and watch as the show comes to life in his living room. The Drowsy Chaperone tells the story of Janet van de Graaff, a famous showgirl who has chosen to leave behind her life of performing for marriage. This show within a show has it all: gangsters, booze, mistaken identities, planes on stage, a British butler and more. So don't miss your chance to see this wonderful production!
